🇮🇳 National Standard Examination in Physics

The first stage of the Indian Physics Olympiad Program to identify talented students and select candidates for the International Physics Olympiad (IPhO)

Register Now

📌 Quick Facts

Organized by

IAPT (Indian Association of Physics Teachers)

Target

Students in Class 11 & 12 (Science stream)

When

Held annually in November

Medium

English & regional languages

Website

iapt.org.in

Watch this video to get a comprehensive overview of the NSEP examination, preparation tips, and success stories from past participants.

🧑‍🎓 Eligibility

  • Age: Born on or after July 1, 2005 (for 2025)
  • Education: Not enrolled in university; currently in Class 11 or 12 (or equivalent)
  • Nationality: Must be an Indian citizen
  • School type: Any board (CBSE, ISC, State Boards, etc.)

📝 Exam Pattern

Section No. of Qs Marks per Q Negative Marking
Part A (MCQ) 48 1 No
Part B (Advanced MCQ) 12 3 −1 for wrong

Total Marks: 240

Duration: 2 hours

Question Type: Conceptual and application-based questions

📚 Syllabus

NSEP is not just a school-level exam; it tests depth of understanding and problem-solving skills.

Covers:

Mechanics

Electricity & Magnetism

Optics

Thermodynamics

Waves & Oscillations

Modern Physics

Units, Errors, Data Analysis

Aligned broadly with CBSE Class 11 & 12, but goes deeper.

✅ Qualifying Criteria for INPhO

To qualify from NSEP to INPhO:

  • Score ≥ Minimum Admissible Score (MAS)
  • Be among top 1% nationwide or qualify through State Quota (SWQ)
  • Final cutoff depends on performance & regional representation

🎯 Benefits of Appearing for NSEP

Entry to IPhO

🚀 Entry point to IPhO, one of the most prestigious science competitions globally

College Applications

🎓 Boosts college applications (IISc, IITs, NISER, IISERs recognize Olympiad achievers)

Fellowships

💼 Recognized by national fellowships and foreign universities

Conceptual Clarity

📖 Deepens conceptual clarity in physics—useful for IIT-JEE, NEET, and SAT Subject Tests

Scientific Thinking

🧠 Encourages scientific thinking and research interest
